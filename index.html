Lines

There are a total of 5 lines. In music we use the lines and spaces to identify which notes to play. There are 7 notes which include A, B, C, D, E, F ,G the order will change depending on which clef you're using.

In percussion and sometimes in band you'll see just one line instead of 5. This is just for counting rhythms and doesn't have specific notes. Each note has a value depending on what the time signature is. You'll learn more about this in the Time signature lesson!
